=== Frequently Asked Questions ===

= How quickly can I get started? =
Just activate the plugin on your website and get started within minutes. [Watch this video](https://youtu.be/X-GAtklyRAs)

= How easy is it for customers to submit a video? =
Your customers can record or upload video from any device without login.

= How many video testimonials can I collect? =
You can collect up to 5 video reviews for free. For more, you need to upgrade the plan.

= Is there any time limit for each video? =
Yes! Every video has a limit of up to 10 minutes (free credits have a limit of up to 2 minutes). It's more than enough to cover the whole review recording. You can set the range of video recording time as per your wish within the time limit.

= What happens if I go over my limit? =
If you go over your limit, we will continue processing video/audio for you, however, you will be required to upgrade.

= Can my customers submit reviews in text? =
Your customers can add text with the video reviews. You just simply have to add some fields along with the video recorder from settings. 

= Is there any short-code? =
Yes, we do have two different short-codes to render the recorder Button and Showcase your testimonials. [Watch this video to learn more](https://youtu.be/upo2QxBRrW0).

= How can I embed the video on my site? =
Yes! We provide embed code for each video. It's just a one-line code. Click on the Share icon bottom of the video and click on the Code icon, you will find the code. You just copy the code and place it anywhere you want.

= Do I need to know any programming language for using this plugin? =
No. Easy Video Reviews is a very user-friendly and simple plugin. It's a plug-and-play type plugin, just download and use it accordingly.

= Will I own the videos I collect? =
With premium plans, YES.

= Can I download the video review? =
Yes! You can download all videos in WEBM format then you can convert the video to any format you wish. You will have a single page to manage all reviews for each of your products.

= What happens to my video if I cancel the account? =
If you want to cancel, we recommend that you download your videos before canceling the service. But your videos will still remain in your canceled account and will be there if you re-subscribe to our service within 3 months.

= What information can I collect from my customer? =
Using the custom form builder option, you can collect different types of information from your customers, such as - name, email, phone number, address, etc.

= My customer can‚Äôt record videos =
Make sure you have installed SSL on your website, otherwise your customer may not be able to record video properly. If you are testing this plugin on your local machine, please browse the website with https:// to bypass the SSL issue. 

If your customers get any error messages on the recorder page, please follow the list to solve the issue:

Please visit our [Youtube playlist](https://youtube.com/playlist?list=PLd6WEu38CQSxMA9pvXN---uJYhwH9UwCw) where we put all the videos of how you can use the features of the plugin. Also, let us know if there is anything we can help you with.
